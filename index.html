The Idea

Designed for your iPhone/iPad to create the ULTIMATE rock climbing experience. Climb your way through routes and explore the outdoors! Collect points and avoid obstacles such as giant spiders, falling rocks, and treacherous caves while going for some serious crimps and holds.
Send it!

Target Audience

iPhone and iPad users. 6+ years old.

Device and System Requirements

As for now, the app will only be available to use on iOS, so you must own an iPhone or an iPad to run 'Send It'. The plan is to write the app in Swift 4.0 as that is the current standard for writing iOS apps. The app will only store local data for highscores and will not require any sort of server to facilitate this. Likely we will only need to use UserDefaults.
